Steps to be followed to give a complaint about yourself or others

  1. Firstly you need to login.if you don't have a account then register and login into our website.
  2. Click on the complaint form to give a complaint.
  3. Fill out the fields that are given in the complaint form.
  4. Check your complaints that you have given.
  5. For police confirmation check the police confirmation field.
  6. If any queries about complaint or others you can contact us
